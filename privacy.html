Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-18

This application ("the App") is a personal automation tool used by its owner to generate short-form video content and upload it to the owner's own TikTok account via the TikTok Content Posting API. This policy explains how the App handles information.

Information We Collect

The App does not collect, store, or share personal information from third parties or end users. It operates locally on the owner's computer. To publish content, the App stores an OAuth access token for the owner's own TikTok account locally on the owner's device; this token is never shared with anyone else.

How Information Is Used

The TikTok access token is used solely to upload video content to the owner's own TikTok account. The App does not read, collect, or process data belonging to other TikTok users.

Data Sharing

The App does not sell, rent, or share any data with third parties. Video content is transmitted only to TikTok for the purpose of posting to the owner's account, subject to TikTok's own terms and privacy policy.

Data Retention

Access tokens and generated content are stored locally on the owner's device and can be deleted by the owner at any time.
